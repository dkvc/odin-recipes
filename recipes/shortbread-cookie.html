Shortbread Sugar Cookie Bars

Description

These shortbread sugar cookie bars are an easy, tasty variation on the traditional cookie.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup confectioners' sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon sugar, or to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix flour, butter, confectioners' sugar, salt, and baking powder together in a bowl using an electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Pat dough into a 9-inch square baking pan. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden, 30 to 35 minutes. Cut into bars while still warm.

Notes

Feel free to use maragine, if you prefer.

Nutritional Facts

Per Serving: 180 calories; protein 1.7g; carbohydrates 17.4g; fat 11.7g; cholesterol 30.5mg; sodium 126mg.
