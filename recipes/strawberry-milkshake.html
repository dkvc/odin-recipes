Strawberry Milkshake

Return to Home

Description

A creamy and sweet strawberry milkshake made with real strawberries.

Ingredients

  • 13 fresh strawberries
  • 4 scoops strawberry ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Â½ cup ice
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream, or to taste
  • 2 teaspoons chocolate sauce, or to taste

Directions

  1. Blend strawberries, ice cream, milk, honey, and vanilla extract together in an electric blender. Blend in ice.
  2. Pour into glasses, squirt on whipped cream, and drizzle chocolate sauce on top.

Nutritional Facts

Per Serving: 305 calories; protein 7g; carbohydrates 52.8g; fat 8.4g; cholesterol 28.9mg; sodium 97.1mg
